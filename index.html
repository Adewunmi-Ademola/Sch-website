Our Story

Successlane Academy was established in September 2018. It began with full operation as Nursery and Primary section. The proprietress with a school staff began the setting with a four Pupils.

Over the years, the school has steadily grown in both size and reputation. Throughout its history, the school has maintained a strong commitment to Academic excellence and character development.

Today Successlane Academy continues to stand as a symbol of educational excellence in the area. We are adapting to meet the ever-changing needs of our pupils and preparing them for a bright future.
